Biography

Roger Federer

  • On the 8th August 1981 Roger was born to Robert and Lynette Federer
  • Roger began playing Tennis at the age of six in Basel, Switzerland
  • Stephan Edberg and Boris Becker were two of his childhood idols growing up
  • Roger met his wife Mirka in Sydney during the olympic games in 2000
  • Mirka and Roger have two sets of twins; Myla and Charlene, Lenny and Leo
  • Roger turned professional in 1998 after winning the boy's singles title at Wimbledon that same year
  • Throughout his Tennis career Roger has won 20 Grand Slams and is the curent record holder
  • Roger's current world ranking is number 4
